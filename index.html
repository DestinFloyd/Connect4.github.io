How to Play?

All you need is two Players! Each player alternates clicking, which places a piece on the board. The pieces fall straight down, occupying the lowest available space within the column. The objective of the game is to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own discs. The first player to connect four pieces wins!
